Privacy Policy for Vyoma: AR Cosmic Atlas

Last Updated: February 19, 2026

Introduction

Vyoma: AR Cosmic Atlas ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • Purpose: We collect your device's location (GPS coordinates) to provide accurate astronomical object positioning and AR features based on your geographical location.
  • Type: Fine and coarse location data
  • Usage: Location data is used only while the app is in use to calculate celestial object positions relative to your location.
  • Storage: Location data is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to external servers or stored permanently.

Sensor Data

  • Accelerometer and Compass: We access your device's accelerometer and compass sensors to enable AR features and determine device orientation for accurate sky mapping.
  • Usage: Sensor data is processed in real-time on your device to provide AR visualization.
  • Storage: Sensor data is not stored or transmitted outside your device.

Internet Access

  • Purpose: Internet connectivity may be used for future features or updates.
  • Current Usage: The app primarily functions offline using pre-loaded astronomical data.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • User accounts or login credentials
  • Payment information
  • Browsing history or app usage analytics
  • Photos or media files
  • Contact lists
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes

How We Use Your Information

Your location and sensor data are used exclusively to:

  • Display accurate positions of celestial objects in the sky
  • Provide AR visualization of astronomical objects
  • Calculate rise/set times and visibility of celestial bodies
  • Enhance your stargazing experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All data processing occurs locally on your device
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers
  • No user data is stored in cloud services or databases
  • The app does not create user profiles or track user behavior

Third-Party Services

Vyoma: AR Cosmic Atlas does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed to be educational and safe for all ages.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny location permissions (though this will limit AR functionality)
  • Uninstall the app at any time, which removes all associated data
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect or store personal data on external servers, there is no data retention period. All data exists only temporarily during app usage on your local device.

International Users

The app processes all data locally on your device, regardless of your location. No data is transferred internationally.
